Figma Files

Companion Figma files on Figma Community.

Each file is a number system — a different answer to the same question: where do the values in your design system actually come from?

Three systems, three different answers. All free on Figma Community.

Golden Ratio

φ = 1.618

Built on the golden ratio, applied from a base of 8px. Every spacing value is mathematically connected to the next. The same proportional logic found in nature, architecture, and music. Not a style, a foundation.

Open in Figma

Typographic Ratio

Classical type scale

Built on the classical type scale. Sizes that have been used in print, on screens, in every medium type has ever lived in. They don't need justifying. They arrive already related to each other, accumulated over centuries of people working out what the eye finds natural.

Open in Figma

Base 10

Multiples of 10

Round numbers in multiples of 10, with mid-steps for finer control. Simple values that are easy to apply consistently. Sometimes the math is just clarity.
